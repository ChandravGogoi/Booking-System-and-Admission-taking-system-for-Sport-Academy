Welcome To UPPER ASSAM SHUTTLERS ACADEMY

Established in 2010, UPPER ASSAM SHUTTLERS ACADEMY has been the home of badminton excellence for over a decade. Our state-of-the-art facility boasts 3 professional-grade courts, advanced lighting systems, and top-notch amenities to ensure the best playing experience for both amateur enthusiasts and professional athletes.

We host regular tournaments, training camps, and workshops led by international badminton coaches and trainees. Our mission is to promote the sport of badminton and nurture the next generation of champions.
